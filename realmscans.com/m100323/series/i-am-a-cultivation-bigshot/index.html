Synopsis I am a Cultivation Bigshot

Li Nianfan descended into the world of immortal cultivation with a mortal body. After learning that there is no hope for immortal cultivation, he just wants to live his life in peace. But unknowingly—

Because of his poetry and painting, the dog be adopted, became a generation of demon king, suppressing a world.
Because of the piano and music he played, the tree he planted behind his house became the world tree, supporting the bridge between heaven and earth.
And because of his casual enlightenment, a passerby he met, became an immortal sage, leading an era…
